About Philons

Ssenyonga Philip AKA Philons is a Ugandan male artist born in 1999 in Kampala, Uganda.

Philons is an exceptionally talented artist who has captured the hearts of music lovers with his unique blend of soulful melodies, thought-provoking lyrics, and captivating performances...

Born and raised in a small town, Philons discovered his passion for music at a tender age, surrounded by a family of musicians and artists.
